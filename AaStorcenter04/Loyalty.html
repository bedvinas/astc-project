Loyalty Points

You can scan reciepts from partnering stores to obrain loyalty points. These can be spent on various benefits to enhance your shopping experience.

Voting

Spend your points to vote on a weekly selection of items. The item with most votes will receive a special discount in the following week.

Charity

Loyalty Ranks

The more points you spend, the higher your Loyalty Rank. These ranks give passive benefits, like additional discounts at the stores.
